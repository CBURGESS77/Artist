Hello! My name is Carlos Burgess. I'm an artist located in Virginia Beach, VA. I've basically been drawing all my life. I haven't done any exhibitions or showcased my work at any art galleries (too shy I guess). However, I'm setting up this site to show you some of my art work and my cartoon strips to which I'm having a lot of fun working on. I don't have much formal art training because I was too busy serving in the Army, and working as a technician at the CDC when I was living in Atlanta. I'm also currently dealing with chronic kidney failure to which can be a drag. However, you can read more about that in my cartoon blog page. Thanks for coming to my site and enjoy!
